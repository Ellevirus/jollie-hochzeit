Our Wedding Venue

We are thrilled to celebrate our special day at the beautiful Rosengarten Estate. This enchanting venue offers a perfect blend of rustic charm and elegant sophistication, providing a romantic backdrop for our wedding celebration.

Nestled in the heart of the countryside, Rosengarten Estate boasts lush gardens, a picturesque lake, and a stunning historic mansion. The ceremony will take place in the rose garden, followed by a reception in the beautifully restored barn.

Address: 1234 Rosengarten Way, Weddingville, 12345
